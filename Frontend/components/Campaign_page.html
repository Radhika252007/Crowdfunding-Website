About the Fundraiser

In the bustling city of Lucknow, thousands of children living in slum areas dream of going to school, but poverty, lack of resources, and social challenges keep them away from classrooms. For many of them, education is a distant hope rather than a daily reality.

Our initiative is working to change that. By creating community classrooms inside the heart of these neighborhoods, we provide children with a safe, supportive space to learn. With volunteer teachers, donated books, basic stationery, and digital learning aids, these classrooms become more than just places of study – they become a doorway to opportunity.

Why This Matters
Breaking the cycle of poverty: Education gives children the chance to pursue better jobs and futures.
Safe spaces to learn: Instead of wandering streets, children gain structured time in a caring environment.
Confidence and self-worth: Learning helps children believe in their potential and value their voices.

Last Updated: August 18, 2025

Anjali Verma
We are thrilled to share that our community classroom in Lucknow’s Indira Nagar slum has taken another big step forward. This week, we introduced structured learning sessions with the help of three volunteer teachers who are dedicating their time daily to teach reading, writing, and basic arithmetic. Over 65 children attended the sessions, many of them stepping into a classroom environment for the very first time. Alongside lessons, we also distributed school bags and notebooks so that every child could start fresh with their own learning materials. Parents have begun attending short orientation meetings, which is helping us build stronger trust and continuity in the program. Our next focus will be to introduce digital aids like a projector for interactive lessons, ensuring children remain engaged and inspired to learn. Each contribution from supporters like you is making this dream a reality.
